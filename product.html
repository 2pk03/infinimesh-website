Industrial IoT - what does it mean?
Although the Internet of Things (IoT) seems a hard concept to grasp at first the underlying idea is simple: What IoT means is that objects with digital capabilities are enabled to sense, communicate, interact and collaborate with the world and so become part of an internet of physical devices, things. Lately, this the trend to make devices connected and smart has progressed faster than ever and forced itself to the top of the tech agenda. With this speed, over 40 billion devices will be online in a few years.

One characteristic of the Industrial IoT is the development of smart products, sensors and actors. Operating and maintain smart product entails new complex infrastructures, concepts and operational models. IoT-based Business Models need to support reliable communication, independent software updates or permission management, to name a few. The effort to do this can be enormous. infinimesh IoT speeds up product development and enables go-to-market at predictable cost.

What is the infinimesh IoT Platform?
We believe smart and connected devices bring our society forward. Smart technology uses resources only when they are really necessary and so prevents waste. On the other hand, when really required, smart things act and hence prevent accidents or simply enable a great user experience. We have started infinimesh 100% Open Source, without patents or closed software. Any software components we have developed, and to this we commit going forward, will be open - forever. Founded by engineers who built the backbone of the European energy revolution, infinimesh aims to make industrial and individual IoT secure, available and affordable for all.





What makes us different

Asset Management

infinimesh gives you quick and deep insights about all IoT assets managed - be they managed over gateways, edge devices or directly connected - never lose any information what your fleet is doing!
IoT and IIoT operation at scale require transparency and insights about and into the managed fleet. infinimesh has developed a graph-based organizing framework with real-time device statistics. Arrange your devices in an overview which reflects your organisational or project based set-up, manage which device belongs where, manage groups on different layers and define who has access as RBAC model. To make this fully secure, we provide granular access management up to device level. IoT operations have never been easier!

IoT Security

Building new IoT or IIoT products also needs to have manageable security mechanisms in place. Starting at the factory provisioning, knowing which device was built with the right certificate is mandatory for strategic implementations. Trust is the most important factor in a valuable IoT business chain. See what the infinimesh IoT Platform can provide and help to build up a trust of devices.
We use military-grade RSA 512 keys to guarantee secure message transmission, even over uncertain and unstable WAN connectivity like LoRa, SigFox, WhiteNoise or Satellite. Our MQTT broker accept only certificates which are known by the signed CA and make sure that not yet known, but trusted devices can send data into a dedicated secure queue - never loose any information during long rollout phases.
