Industrial IoT - what does it mean?
Although the Internet of Things (IoT) seems to be pretty complicated, the underlying idea is rather straightforward. In short words IoT means that objects with embedded technologies are capable of sensing, communicating, interacting and collaborating with other objects and in that way build a network of physical things. Lately, this concept underwent an extremely fast-paced progress and thus reached the top of the tech agenda. If this speed is kept up there is a forecast of approximately 26 billion connected devices within the next years.

One characteristic of the Industrial IoT is the development of smart products, sensors and actors. Operating and maintain smart product entails new complex infrastructures, concepts and operational models. IoT-based Business Models need to support reliable communication, independend software updates or permission management, to name a few. The effort to do this can be enormous. Infinimesh IoT speeds up product development and enables go-to-market at predictable cost.
