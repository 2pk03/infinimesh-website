Our Mission

IoT will be the next step into a fully connected society - and we think that journey needs to be as easy as possible.
Founded by engineers who built the backbone for the European Energy Revolution infinimesh IoT aims to make Industrial and Individual IoT securely available and affordable for everyone, at every time and everywhere.

Board of Directors and Management

Alexander (President, Founder)

Alex is a technically proficient IIoT, IoT, BigData, Large Scale Platform Architect and advanced software engineer at heart with almost 25 years of experience in complex software design. He is a digital expert and drives the digitalization across the industry, and as a well-known entrepreneur he loves to build cutting edge tech to solve future challenges of our mankind today.
