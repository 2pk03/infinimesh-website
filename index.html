Smart Grid and Intelligent Infrastructures are the backbone of the upcoming Energy Change. With the Infinimesh Platform your long lasting assets are getting intelligent without driving cost and complexity. Monitoring low voltage grids, managing high voltage transformators or performing complex switch scenarios, Infinimesh manages all those data driven models - easily and reliably. Renewables & Energy

Operating renewable energy systems like windmills, solar farms, sun2gas or hydrogen needs a new operating and management model to make use of the large amount generated. Infinimesh enables real data-driven business models and gives the confidence to manage large and costly assets in a cost-efficient and reliable way.

Automotive & Industry

Connected cars, self-driving cars, robotic, autonomous drones, smart cities, interconnected railroads - Infinimesh can power all of them. Build for low latency and high data ingest it lets you create these technology stacks in minutes with just a few commands.
