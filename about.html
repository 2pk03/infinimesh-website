Our Mission
IoT will be the next step into a fully connected society - and we think that journey needs to be as easy as possible.
Founded by engineers who build the backbone for the European Energy Revolution Infinimesh IoT aims to make Industrial and Individual IoT securely available and affordable for everyone, at every time and everywhere.

Alexander Alten (Co-Founder and CEO)

Alex is a technically proficient IIoT, IoT, BigData, Large Scale Platform Architect and advanced software engineer at heart with almost 25 years of experience in complex software design. He’s a digital expert and drives the digitalization across the industry and a well known entrepreneur who loves to build cutting edge tech to solve future challenges our mankind today.

Johannes Bruederl (Co-Founder and CTO)

Johannes is a Software Engineer for life. He enjoys working on distributed systems and open source products. His preferred tech-stack includes Kubernetes, Apache Kafka and Go. He is also an active open source contributor and enjoys fixing bugs & writing enhancements for upstream open source projects. Johannes is an expert in designing distributed systems, programming and implementing systems to be consumed by other developers with a DevOps approach.

Florian Gruenwald (Co-Founder and COO)

Florian is passionate about agile product development: he has taken on Agile transformation initiatives, Coaching of Dev teams and Product Ownership, mostly in web development. He is also a Front End developer, mostly working with JavaScript, vue.js and vuetify and also has some node.js experience. He is keen on making infinimesh a totally customer-centric business.

Romina Medici (SVP Business Development & Strategy)

Romina’s biggest passion is to understand how data can be used to transform whole organizations and cultures. Since almost nine years she is working on various roles which all did have data as their major focus. Starting from being an Analyst in the Supply Chain organization to being a Data Manager and Data Governance expert. In all roles she did transform the organization to be data centric based on analytic skills and strong business knowledge. She is already looking forward to help our customers to go through this transformation and aim to be successful.
